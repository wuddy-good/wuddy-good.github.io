# Mental Health Specialist Connection Platform

A platform that connects specialists in the field of mental health with clients, including psychologists, psychotherapists, and psychiatrists. The platform aims to involve a large number of specialists (2-3 thousand) and provide comprehensive profiles and questionnaires.

## Features

- **Specialist Profiles:** Clients can view all information about psychologists without the need for authorization.
- **Client Authorization:** Authorization occurs during booking, creating a client account and personal account. Clients are authorized by receiving a code on their mobile phone.
- **Booking System:** Clients can book sessions directly on the platform. They can view the therapist's schedule, synchronized with Google Calendar, and select available hours.
- **Contract Agreement:** Clients review and accept a contract outlining the terms of psychotherapeutic assistance before booking.
- **Online Payment:** Clients make payments through online services like Apple Pay, Google Pay, Visa, MasterCard, and PayPal upon booking.
- **Therapist Payment Options:** 
  - Immediate payment after the session
  - Weekly payments
  - Monthly payments
- **Platform Commission:** The platform receives a share of the session cost after deducting the specialist's commission.

## Session Management

- **Google Meet Integration:** Sessions are conducted via Google Meet. Upon booking, a Google Meet meeting is created and displayed in the therapist's calendar.
- **Email Notifications:** Clients and therapists receive emails with session details and a Google Meet link.

## Workflow

1. **Client Views Specialist Profiles:** No authorization required.
2. **Booking and Authorization:** Client books a session, authorizes via mobile code, and creates an account.
3. **Selecting Date and Time:** Client chooses an available slot from the therapist's Google Calendar.
4. **Contract Review:** Client reviews and accepts the terms of service.
5. **Payment:** Client makes payment through the chosen online service.
6. **Session Confirmation:** Google Meet link is sent to both client and therapist via email.
7. **Therapist Receives Payment:** Based on selected payment option, after deducting the platform's commission.
